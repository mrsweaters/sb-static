How it works

It’s never been easier to monitor the visual appearance of your website! Choose regions of your web page to watch and Screenbeacon will notify you if something changes.

Compare Screenshots

Screenbeacon records screenshots of the website’s current appearance to see if the differ from the baseline.


  • Baseline
  • Capture
  • Difference
  • Baseline Image

    The first screenshot you take of a region will be set as the baseline for future tests.

  • Captured Image

    For each time a test is run, Screenbeacon screenshots the region in its current state.

  • Image Difference

    If the capture differs from the baseline, Screenbeacon will produce a diff.

Compare CSS

If the calculated difference of the screenshots passes a certain threshold (5% by default), Screenbeacon will test the computed styles. This helps to eliminate false-positives.

We provide fully automated integration testing for visual design. Ensure that your website always looks the way you intend.
